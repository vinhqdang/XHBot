\section{Conclusion}
In this paper, we presented XHBot, a novel graph neural network framework specifically tailored to counter social bot relation camouflage. By integrating Spectral-Guided Topology Refinement and Tri-Channel Heterophily-Aware Aggregation, our model successfully decouples human noise from bot anomaly signatures. Crucially, the addition of the Hierarchical Forensic Explanation module ensures that detections are paired with actionable, interpretable evidence. Empirical evaluations demonstrate that XHBot significantly outperforms traditional baseline models, securing a massive +27.37\% F1 score improvement in challenging high-heterophily scenarios.
